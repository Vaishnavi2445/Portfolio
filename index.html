About Me

Software Engineer!

I'm a passionate software engineer with a focus on algorithm design and visualization, and I take pride in writing clean and readable code. Over the past two years, I've gained strong proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C/C++, and Java, and I've also worked extensively with technologies like Git, MySQL, and developing APIs. One of my major interests lies in Data Structures and Algorithm Design, where I love tackling complex problems and finding efficient solutions. In fact, I had the opportunity to showcase my skills during Hacktoberfest 2022, where I made quality contributions to open source repositories. It was an enriching experience to explore different codebases and add value to the projects. Aside from my technical pursuits, I've also been recognized for my academic achievements. I was awarded the Nurturing Brilliance Scholarship by Cummins India Foundation, which supports meritorious students from financially and socially challenging backgrounds. Additionally, I'm an active member of the Training & Placement Cell's core team, where I guide students in making informed career choices and facilitate connections between companies and students. Outside of academics, I have a strong commitment to community engagement. As a member of the National Cadet Corps (NCC), I actively participate in social awareness initiatives, environmental causes, disaster relief efforts, and sports activities that contribute to nation-building. Furthermore, I serve as a coordinator for Rudras Dance Crew, where I encourage students to showcase their dance talent and participate in various events both within and outside the campus. I am excited about the challenges that lie ahead in my software engineering journey and look forward to leveraging my skills and experiences to make a positive impact in the field. Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me!
